Townsend Janney, a high school volunteer at Children's National Medical Center, heard about the Treats and Treasures carts and wanted to help. So, she decided to have a bake sale and a toy drive at her school, H-B Woodlawn Secondary Program in Arlington, VA. She enlisted the help of her friends, Erin Morrissey and Allegra Wham. Together, they had a very successful event! We are so thankful! Townsend is standing next to the Treasures Cart filled with the results of her efforts. Not only did she donate a great number of toys, she also presented a check to The Foundation for over $180.00! Townsend will be joining us at EVANfest 2014 where we will have the opportunity to thank her on stage.
