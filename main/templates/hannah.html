10 year old Hannah wanted to do something to raise money for the EVAN Foundation. Hannah knew Evan and wanted to do what she could to honor him and raise money to fight neuroblastoma. On her own, Hannah developed TWO ideas in order to achieve her goal.

First, she really enjoys creating woven wrist bands. She decided that she would offer her custom made creations to raise money for the EVAN Foundation. She sold the colorful wristbands for $2.00 each and made $20.00.

Her second idea involved her creative weaving skills once again. She made Christmas tree ornaments that were sold at a Christmas shop in her school. She approached the principal on her own and asked if she could sell the ornaments in the shop to raise money for the EVAN Foundation. The principal agreed and Hannah earned $50.00!

We extend our warmest gratitude to Hannah for showing such mature initiative and doing her part to help the EVAN Foundation reach our goal!
